What we do

Empowering communities to break the silence and take a stand against child sexual abuse through education, advocacy, and support, fostering a world where every child can grow up free from harm and exploitation.

1

I am the Future

Boys and Girls regardless of their age, race or class are vulnerable to child sexual abuse. According to UNICEF statistics of 2015,1 in 4 girls and 1 in 10 boys experience child sexual abuse. 25% of all girls and 10% of boy's experience sexual violence in Nigeria before the age of 18years. Fewer than five out of a 100 received any form of support.

2

I protect the Future

Conversation around body safety, sexual abuse and age appropiate sex education are often seen as taboos or family matters that "should not" be talked about openly. Sadly abusers and molesters take advanteage of this to sexally abuse children

3

Let's Talk About it

Conversation arond puberty are often seen as taboos,abnormal and a no-go area. PTCF has also launched PTCF TV vox pop series called "let's talk about it" where all topics on age appropriate sex education are talked about by Nigerians on the street,
