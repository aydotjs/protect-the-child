Protect The Child Foundation was at Nigeria info, 95.1, Nigeria Abuja to discuss the impact of Sexual and Gender Based Violence (SGBV) on education in Nigeria to mark the International Day of Education held on the 24th of January 2023 Education is key in transforming any society but unfortunately sexual and gender based violence (SGBV) or school related sexual and gender based violence (SRGBV) can negatively impact education and the society as a whole.
