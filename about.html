About Us

Empowering Futures, Protecting Innocence: Our Commitment to Safeguarding Children and Nurturing Their Potential

Our Mission

Protect the Child Foundation (PTCF) is a Nigerian based Non-Governmental Organization committed to the protection and defence of the innocence of children against all forms of sexual abuse and exploitation.

Our vision is to create a safe childhood free from sexual abuse for the African child.

Our mission is to work towards the eradication of child sexual abuse through the provision of relevant information, emergency, medical, legal and other service that empower, inform and protect the African child.
